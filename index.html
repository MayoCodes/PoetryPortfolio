  • "The weight of
    expectations
    can feel like a
    heavy burden
    at first, but helps
    one grow and become
    resilient over time ."

    Brief Overview

    The statement above is a theme statement regarding the thoughtful poems included in this portfolio.

    Types of poems

    This portfolio is a representation of my own experiences and feelings on the subject of expcetations and includes many forms of poetry such as concrete, double acrostic, free verse, narrative, cinquain, and list poems.

    Purpose

    By reading the following portfolio I hope that I will have been able to convey my message and internal experiences to you successfully. Thank you for reading.
